If you want to see what a real single speed is, I think this is the bike to look at. Chijioke is a professional BMX rider for MacNeil, he has an incredible amount of experience as a technician in shops, and is hands down the best wheel builder I have ever heard of. His riding experience includes trials, mountain biking, and of course an impressive amount of BMX. As you could imagine he is a very picky person when it comes to bikes, and doesn't like to settle for less than perfection.

After a ton of brain storming we came up with a solid set of geometry, and he assembled his extremely unique part spec. He needed the bike for practical riding throughout the city, and that is exactly what we ended up with. Setup with Thomson stem and post, Chris King hubs and headset, and a spline drive 39T Tree sprocket with Profile cranks makes this bike more than an eye piece. A set of Eclat levers and Thomson bars make his cockpit not something you see everyday. Chijioke chose to top it off with Laguna Seca Blue, it's not like him to have something that isn't all black, but we both agreed that this is better than any black colour scheme could have been.

This bike is an absolute street destroyer, and a bike you can proudly put on the wall at home. By building this I paid my due homage to Chijioke for teaching me everything I know about bicycles to date, he is a truly versed and well round rider.
