How To Order

Unfortunately when most people talk about bespoke frames, they are always shocked at the unattainable price for a custom frame. Being an Aircraft Structures Technician I have access to my own personal shop, as well as the inedible tooling and space at my place of work, which can keep my price much lower than the norm for frames. Riding a product from a factory hinders your ride and isn't comparable to a tailored bicycle. I keep my costs low solely so riders do have the option to break into the handmade market at a similar price to a store bought frame.

As every frame is unique, there are no set prices. You can see a list of my price estimates, though a more accurate quote can be obtained by sending an email.
