Patrick Gauci

The bicycle is the most efficient form of human movement. Whether it is working, commuting, racing, or leisure riding many people rely on the i bicyce, but thats not why I think people ride them. They do it to find their own personal enjoyment and satisfaction. The bicycles I make are designed to give each person a unique experience by riding something that is truly tailored to their every need.

Simplicity is the key to the design and construction of my bikes and by crafting every element by hand I am able to able to have complete control of the outcome of every product that leaves my shop. Giving each customer a ride that brings them complete happiness and a feeling no other bike has brought them before is my one and only goal with Patrick Cycle Works.
