Christie’s Road Frame

Liam Parker first came and asked me if I could build him a mountain frame to replace his Storck Rebel Nine. After talking about that for a bit, he ended up deciding that this effort could be better spent on his soon to be, and now, wife Christie as a wedding present. Liam has a long history of working in shops, the most recent of which is running Bikes On Wheels. His love of cycling is clearly shown by the incredible amount of riding he does, and his past experience in almost every category of cycling. Something I have learned from Liam is how important simplicity is, and how nothing is more ascetically pleasing than a simple but well carried out design. He gave me a set of geometry and a few ideas to work with. Classic road geometry, and design was key for him. He showed his mountain biking roots by choosing a similar Teal to what Yeti uses on there bikes. Parts are still being assembled for this, but don’t expect to see anything less than top of line. I know Enve and Zipp are on the list for this bike. Check out what a real Canadian road machine rides like at 28teeth.tumblr.com
